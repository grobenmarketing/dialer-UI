1. The Opener

LAST UPDATED: 2024-03-15 // CLEARANCE: ALL AGENTS

The goal of the opener is not to pitch. It is to lower resistance. When you hear "Hello," do not jump into a pitch.

THE SCRIPT:
"Hi John, it's [Name]... I'll be honest, I'm calling you out of the blue, do you have a moment or did I catch you at a terrible time?"

By admitting it's a cold call ("out of the blue"), you disarm the "Salesman Alarm" in their brain.
